Troubleshooting

From time to time, default weather stations may not be active. This is usually evident as a current temperature value of -99 °F . There are two (2) places in the app where -99 °F may appear.

  • Map View
  • Forecast View
In both cases the technique for selecting another weather station is the same.

Station Selection

The steps described will follow the sequence outlined in Figure 1 - Troubleshooting.

Step 1.

Erroneous weather data is identified in the Map View or Forecast View.

Step 2.

Select the app Settings tab to access the Settings View.

Step 3.

Identify the Current Weather Source section and select Set Weather Station.

Step 4.

Scroll up in the list of weather stations and make a new selection.

Step 5.

Select the Forecast View tab.

Step 6.

Tap the current temperature icon.

Step 7.

A corrected temperature value should now be shown. If the selected weather station does not provide a new temperature value, repeat the steps to select a new station.

Alert Schedule

By default, alert schedule is set to Auto. Auto sets the alert update frequrency based on the severity of the forecast weather sky condition. For example, a tornado forecast sets the alert update frequrency to 30 minutes while sunny conditions sets it to 6 hours. The list of selectable alert options are as follows:

  • 1 hour
  • 2 hours
  • 4 hours
  • 8 hours
  • 12 hours
Alert update frequency can be set in the iPhone settings for the WxAlert app.
